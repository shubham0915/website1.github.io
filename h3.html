ECOSYSTEM DISRUPTION:

Climate change leads to disruptions in ecosystems, impacting the distribution and behavior of plant and animal species. Shifts in temperature and precipitation patterns can result in altered habitats and threaten the balance of various ecosystems.

ENDANGERED SPECIES:

CClimate change poses a significant threat to various species, especially those with specific habitat requirements. The changing climate can affect breeding patterns, food sources, and migration routes, putting many species at risk of extinction.
